About the Program

A once-in-a-generation opportunity

For 117 years, Cairo University has been quietly building one of the world's most extraordinary human networks — 1.5 million graduates who lead nations, found industries, advance science, and shape culture across Africa, the Arab World, and beyond. Until now, that network had no home. The Cairo University Alumni Program changes that.

The Cairo University Alumni Program is more than an initiative — it is Cairo University's promise to its 1.5 million graduates: that their relationship with this institution did not end at graduation. It calls them back to the university that made them, and channels their collective wisdom, capital, and mentorship toward the leaders Egypt will need next. It transforms alumni from passive graduates into active architects of CU's future: mentors who open doors, investors who back bold ideas, philanthropists who leave lasting legacies, and ambassadors who carry Cairo University's name to every corner of the world.

This is not a fundraising campaign. It is a homecoming.

Alumni Benefits

Give back. Receive more.

Cairo University's alumni program doesn't just invite you back — it invests in you. Benchmarked against the world's most elite universities, the CUAP benefit framework is designed to deliver real, tangible, lifelong value to every graduate who steps forward. Whether you are a recent graduate finding your footing or a global leader at the height of your career, the program meets you where you are and grows with you.

Benefits span three powerful dimensions — identity and recognition that announces who you are to the world, professional and career access that keeps opening doors long after graduation, and investment opportunities that put you at the heart of Egypt's most exciting innovation ecosystem.

And because the greatest relationships are built on reciprocity, CUAP operates on a principle as old as Cairo University itself: the more you give, the more you receive. A tiered membership structure ensures every level of engagement is met with a matching level of value — from the member just beginning their journey to the pioneer whose transformative contribution shapes the university for generations. Your commitment unlocks your benefits. Your investment determines your impact.

Your membership begins with something timeless: a recognised, verifiable identity that connects you to one of Africa's greatest institutions — for life.
