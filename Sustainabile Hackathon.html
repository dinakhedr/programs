A series of sustainability-focused hackathons designed to generate practical, market-ready solutions for environmental and industrial challenges. Problem Statement

While “green entrepreneurship” interest surges among Egyptian youth, many solutions lack technical viability or market relevance.

  • Innovation Gap: Startups often fail due to shallow understanding of industrial sustainability pain points.
  • Technical Validation: Ideas lack rigorous grounding required in regulated sectors (energy, waste).
  • Access to Support: Innovators struggle to find specialized mentorship for green business models.

Strategic Alignment

  • Economic Growth: Fuels green SME sector & economic diversification.
  • Private Sector Engagement: Demand-driven problems from industries.
  • Donor Priorities: Aligns with climate innovation, digitization & entrepreneurship mandates.

🎯 Program Objectives

Solution Generation

Produce 15-20 viable prototypes or PoC solutions addressing specific sustainability challenges — fundable as standalone or graduation projects.

Venture Creation

Support formation of 5-10 new startup teams with market-ready concepts and strong technical foundations.

Market Linkage

Connect innovators directly with corporate partners seeking operational & supply chain solutions.

Capacity Building

Enhance technical & business acumen of 100+ participants via intensive mentorship & workshops.

⚙️ Methodology — 4‑Phase Innovation Sprint

01
Challenge Definition

Problem sourcing with corporate partners. ACTS develops technical briefs, background papers & datasets to guide teams.

02
Outreach & Formation

Basharsoft recruits multidisciplinary talent (devs, engineers, business). Facilitated matchmaking ensures balanced skills.

03
Hackathon (3‑day Sprint)

Hybrid/physical event. Mentorship from ACTS technical experts + industry leaders. Teams build prototypes or detailed roadmaps.

04
Incubation & Handover

Judging by investors & experts. Winners receive "Concept Validation" package, visibility on Basharsoft platforms, Demo Day & investor connections.

Mentorship layers: Technical (environmental feasibility, compliance) + Business (market viability, scalability) + Prototyping support.
