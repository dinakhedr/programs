About Act Sustainable (ACTS)

Act Sustainable is a leading sustainability advisory and capacity-building organization dedicated to accelerating the green transition in the MENA region. With deep expertise in ESG frameworks, carbon accounting, circular economy, and climate strategy, ACTS bridges the gap between sustainability theory and industrial practice. As technical partner for both programs, ACTS designs curricula, validates technical solutions, and provides expert mentorship to ensure market-ready outcomes aligned with Egypt Vision 2030 and global climate standards.
