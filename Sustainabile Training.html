🌿 Executive Summary

A strategic partnership between Basharsoft (WUZZUF/Forasna) and Act Sustainable to address workforce readiness in Egypt’s green economy. We design and deliver technical training programs tailored to real‑time market needs, leveraging employment data and sustainability expertise. The initiative equips university students and fresh graduates with specific technical and operational skills required by employers in energy, waste management, and sustainable manufacturing — creating a verified candidate pool for immediate recruitment.

Labor Market Context

Misalignment between academic outputs and private sector sustainability needs.

  • Supply gap: Curricula lack practical green tech, ESG compliance & CBAM/EU standards.
  • Demand pressure: Employers face sustainability mandates but cannot find verified talent.
  • Data insight: Growing green vacancies with low fulfillment rates due to unverified qualifications.

Strategic Alignment

  • Egypt Vision 2030: Strengthens “Integrated & Sustainable Ecosystem” pillar.
  • Climate Action: Formalizes green job functions & supports national climate commitments.
  • Donor priorities: Youth employment, private sector development & climate resilience.

🎯 Program Objectives

Skills Development

Develop & implement technical training curricula based on live job vacancy data in green sectors — solar PV, waste logistics, ESG reporting, carbon accounting.

Employment Facilitation

Train a pilot cohort of 500 university students & graduates with verified competencies, ready for recruitment.

Digital Infrastructure

Integrate skills verification mechanism on WUZZUF/Forasna platforms, enabling employers to instantly identify qualified candidates.

Placement Targets

Secure employment or structured internships for 60% of program graduates within six months of completion.

⚙️ Methodology — 4‑Phase Implementation

01
Market Assessment & Curriculum Design

Basharsoft analyzes platform search trends & employer data; ACTS conducts key informant interviews. Joint development of competency frameworks & training materials validated by corporate review committee.

02
Candidate Selection

Outreach via Basharsoft’s database (engineering, science, agriculture, business). Technical aptitude tests + sustainability awareness assessment ensure right fit for green tracks.

03
Training Implementation

Intensive 6–8 week technical tracks + professional development. Site visits & real-world case studies from industry partners. Delivered by ACTS subject matter experts.

04
Placement & Verification

Final assessments grant digital badges on WUZZUF profiles. Exclusive hiring events & structured internships bridge to full-time employment.

Industry-validated learning: Each training module is stress-tested by corporate partners to ensure alignment with hiring standards & emerging EU regulations (CBAM, ESG frameworks).
